    About Us

    “We aim to amplify the voice of every child and woman who is in urgent need of assistance.”

    Aashray Society is a non-governmental organization working towards the welfare of the underprivileged in the society. We work in the field of education, health, and women empowerment. Our aim is to provide a better future for the children and women of our society.

    Our Work

    We believe that small contributions can have a great impact on certain communities deprived of even the most basic necessities for their well-being.

    We envision a society where every person, in spite of the limitations of poverty, has equal opportunities to promote her well-being and achieve her aspirations. We are especially inclined to preserve the childhood of children and empowering women belonging to these communities.

    Our Focus Areas

    Migrant Education

    Migrant Education

    We work to enable learning opportunities for children (6 to 14 years) by tackling challenges and breaks to their education, posed by poverty due to migration to urban spaces.

    Early Childcare

    Early Childcare

    Assisting healthy and satisfactory growth of children (2-6 years) while focusing on their special needs presented by their families’ adverse economic and social status.

    Education4All

    Education4All

    Providing windows of targeted education facilities and skill development for girls, adolescents, young women, children whose education has been greatly hampered.

    Empowering Women

    Empowering Women

    Enhancing the capabilities of women in the communities through education, vocational trainings, counselling, family meetings.

    Health Education

    Health Education

    Spreading awareness, especially amongst the women in the communities about the benefits of a healthy upbringing for children.

    Youth Education

    Youth Education

    We work with kids to bridge the gap into higher education with vocational skills and certifications.
