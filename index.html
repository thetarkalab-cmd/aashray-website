About Us

“We aim to amplify the voice of every child and woman who is in urgent need of assistance.” Aashray Society is an NGO registered under the Indian Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860. It is currently based in Noida, Uttar Pradesh. At Aashray Society, we are dedicated to the cause of creating and implementing sustainable projects to assist vulnerable and underprivileged children and women in India. We aspire to bring joy through learning in their lives, particularly, through education, nutrition, life skills, health care and hygiene and through informative activities.

Our Work

We believe that small contributions can have a great impact on certain communities deprived of even the most basic necessities for their well-being. We envision a society where every person, in spite of the limitations of poverty, has equal opportunities to promote her well-being and achieve her aspirations. We are especially inclined to preserve the childhood of children and empowering women belonging to these communities.

Our Projects

Our initiatives focus on long-term support for children and families through education, care, and community-based programs.

Project Aashray Shiksha
Supporting access to education for children from migrant and underprivileged families through informal learning spaces and academic support.

Early Childhood Care Programme
Focused on nutrition, health, and early learning for young children in vulnerable urban communities.

Women & Family Support Initiative
Working with mothers and caregivers to strengthen family well-being, awareness, and self-reliance.
