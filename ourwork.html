Project Saksham

Project Saksham is our main initiative through which Aashray Society works with children and families from vulnerable migrant communities, focusing on education, care, and long-term development. We have been working with a small group of children and families for almost a decade to bring about sustained change in the lives of marginalised migrant workers.

Our projects have a strong focus on nutrition, health, and early learning for young children in vulnerable urban communities.

We also work with mothers and caregivers to strengthen family well-being, awareness, and self-reliance.

Please reach out to us to know more and contribute to our projects.
